Economy architecture:

Payment distribution mechanism

Burning + reserve

Step 1:
Payment decomposition

N (100% JPORK) = N1 (80%) + N2 (20%)

Step 2:
Fate of each part:

N1 (80%) → Burn address
Tokens are destroyed forever → total supply decreases → creates deflationary pressure.

N2 (20%) → Cold Storage Reserve for market stabilization. Sold ONLY when demand is abnormally high → smoothes price spikes.

Stabilization mechanism

TON Fee → JPORK

How TON commissions work:

N (100% JPORK) = N1 (80%) + N2 (20%)

Logic:

1. All network TON commissions (gas) accumulate in a special insurance fund.

2. In case of sharp volatility (JPORK price drop > set threshold) the smart contract automatically:

Buys JPORK for accumulated TON
Supports the price by adding liquidity
This is a built-in market-maker against panic sales.
